# Chronic-obstructive-pulmonary-disease-COPD-study
A study to determine the effects of long-term exposure to air pollution on the development of chronic obstructive pulmonory disease (COPD) among the US population.
This study starts first with the cleaning of 3 available datasets to make them analysis ready. These dataset contain background characteristics of the study participants, residential information for the past 20 years and electronic health records that have history of the diagnosis of COPD and other diseases.
The datasets were independently cleaned before merging into a composite dataset for analysis, and are available in the dataset folder. The details of every step in the data cleaning are elaborated in the data cleaning plan that accompany this repository and is availble in the documentation folder. The documentation also validates all indicators of quality assurance of analysis-ready dataset, and provides as well as the data dictionary. 
Finally annotated R-scripts accompany this repository to detail the steps used in the data cleaning to make it reproducible and facilitate collaboration. Furhter information is required e.g. air pollution exposure is needed to complete the dataset before analysis. 
Your views and expertise are highly welcome to produce a very robost statistical analysis and inference on this study.
